1. Introduction

This Human Resources (HR) Policy Manual outlines the policies, procedures, and guidelines governing employment at DAR Enterprise. The objective is to ensure a fair, transparent, inclusive, and professional work environment aligned with the company’s values, legal requirements, and business goals.

These policies apply to all employees of DAR Enterprise unless otherwise specified.

2. Employment Philosophy

DAR Enterprise believes in:

Equal opportunity employment

Merit-based growth and recognition

Ethical conduct and professionalism

Respect, integrity, and collaboration

3. Employment Categories

Employees may be classified as:

Full-Time Employees

Part-Time Employees

Contractual / Consultants

Interns / Trainees

Employment terms will be defined in the offer or appointment letter.

4. Recruitment and Selection

Hiring is based on skills, qualifications, experience, and cultural fit.

No discrimination based on gender, age, religion, caste, disability, or background.

Background verification may be conducted before or after hiring.

5. Onboarding and Probation

All new employees will undergo an onboarding process.

Probation period: 3–6 months (as mentioned in the offer letter).

Performance during probation will be evaluated before confirmation.

6. Working Hours and Attendance

Standard working hours: 9 hours per day, including breaks.

Weekly offs: As per company policy or role requirements.

Employees must adhere to attendance and time-tracking systems.

Late arrivals and absenteeism may lead to disciplinary action.

7. Leave Policy
Types of Leave:

Casual Leave (CL)

Sick Leave (SL)

Earned / Privilege Leave (EL/PL)

Maternity / Paternity Leave (as per applicable laws)

Public & Festival Holidays

Leave approval is subject to reporting manager’s consent and business requirements.

8. Compensation and Benefits

Salary structure will be communicated in the offer letter.

Payments will be made monthly.

Statutory benefits such as PF, ESI, gratuity, etc., will be provided as applicable.

Performance-based incentives may be offered.

9. Performance Management

Employees will undergo periodic performance reviews.

Evaluations are based on role objectives, KPIs, and behavior.

Outcomes may include increments, promotions, training, or corrective actions.

10. Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to:

Act ethically and professionally

Maintain confidentiality of company information

Avoid conflicts of interest

Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect

Misconduct may result in disciplinary action up to termination.

11. Workplace Ethics and Anti-Harassment Policy

DAR Enterprise provides a safe and respectful workplace.

Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or bullying

Complaints will be handled confidentially and fairly

An Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) will be constituted where applicable

12. Health, Safety, and Security

Employees must comply with safety guidelines.

The company will provide a safe working environment.

Any accidents or hazards must be reported immediately.

13. IT, Data Protection, and Confidentiality

Company IT resources are for official use.

Unauthorized sharing of data is prohibited.

Employees must comply with data protection and cybersecurity policies.

14. Training and Development

DAR Enterprise encourages continuous learning through:

On-the-job training

Workshops and skill development programs

Leadership and professional development initiatives

15. Separation and Exit Policy

Separation may occur due to resignation, termination, or retirement.

Notice period as per appointment letter

Handover of responsibilities and company assets is mandatory

Full and final settlement will be processed as per policy

16. Disciplinary Action

Disciplinary measures may include:

Verbal or written warnings

Suspension

Termination

Actions will be based on the severity of misconduct and due process.

17. Policy Amendments

DAR Enterprise reserves the right to amend, modify, or withdraw policies at its discretion. Employees will be informed of any changes.
